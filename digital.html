Project A

ChromaSim

The Empathy Simulation Lab

ChromaSim is an immersive simulation platform that helps educators, employers, and policymakers experience the world through different cognitive lenses. By walking in another mind's shoes, users develop the empathy and practical understanding needed to design truly inclusive environments.

  • Interactive cognitive perspective simulations
  • Scenario-based learning for educators and HR teams
  • Open-source modules adaptable to any context
  • Accessibility-first design with multi-modal input/output
  • Research-backed psychological frameworks
  • Community-contributed simulation scenarios

Why Technology Matters

Digital tools can scale empathy in ways that in-person training cannot.

Scalable Empathy

Simulations reach thousands of decision-makers simultaneously, creating cultural shifts at organizational scale.

Safe Exploration

Users can experiment with inclusive practices in a low-stakes environment before applying them in real settings.

Measurable Impact

Data from simulation sessions informs policy recommendations and tracks inclusion progress over time.
