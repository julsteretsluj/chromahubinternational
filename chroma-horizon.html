ChromaHorizon

ChromaHorizon pairs neurodivergent professionals with peer mentors who have navigated similar career transitions. Whether entering the workforce, changing roles, or advocating for accommodations — participants receive personalized, lived-experience guidance through every stage of their professional journey.

Program Capabilities

Career guidance from people who have actually walked the path — not generic HR scripts.

Core

One-on-One Peer Mentorship

Get matched with a mentor who shares your neurotype, industry context, and transition goals. Sessions are async-friendly with clear agendas and optional video.

  • Identity- and industry-aware matching
  • Structured milestone check-ins
  • Sensory-friendly scheduling options
Playbooks

Career Transition Guides

Industry-specific playbooks cover first jobs, role changes, return-to-work, and promotion paths — written by neurodivergent professionals who have done it.

  • Tech, education, healthcare, and more
  • Step-by-step transition timelines
  • Red flags and green flags by sector
Advocacy

Accommodation Coaching

Templates, scripts, and mentor coaching for disclosure conversations, reasonable adjustment requests, and follow-up when employers push back.

  • Request letter templates
  • Manager conversation scripts
  • Escalation pathway guidance
Employers

Neurodivergent-Led Education

Employer sessions led by neurodivergent professionals — not third-party trainers reading slides. Real stories, practical fixes, and Q&A that changes culture.

  • Manager and HR workshop formats
  • Lived-experience panel sessions
  • Custom briefings by organization size
Community

Flexible Support Groups

Join cohort circles on your terms — text-only threads, scheduled voice rooms, or drop-in async channels. No camera required, no forced participation.

  • Topic rooms by transition stage
  • Low-stimulation participation modes
  • Moderated, trauma-informed spaces
Stories

Success Case Library

Read how others navigated interviews, burnout recovery, promotions, and workplace conflicts — anonymized case studies you can learn from before your next move.

  • Searchable by industry and challenge
  • Before-and-after career narratives
  • Contributor credit optional

Who ChromaHorizon Serves

Every professional journey is different — these are the transitions we support most.

Path

Entering the Workforce

First role, first disclosure

From internship to graduate hire — navigate applications, interviews, onboarding, and when (and how) to request accommodations without losing momentum.

Path

Changing Roles

Promotion, pivot, or new employer

Switch industries, level up, or escape a bad fit. Mentors help you evaluate offers, negotiate adjustments, and plan transitions that protect your energy.

Path

Advocating at Work

When the environment needs to change

Build the confidence and language to ask for what you need — and the peer backup when managers, HR, or culture push back.

How ChromaHorizon Works

From signup to sustained support — on your timeline.

1. Match

Share your goals, industry, and communication preferences. We pair you with a mentor who has navigated a similar path.

2. Navigate

Work through playbooks, coaching sessions, and community circles as your transition unfolds — no fixed curriculum pace.

3. Thrive & Give Back

Land your next step, contribute your story to the library, and optionally mentor the next cohort when you are ready.

Peer Power, Not Policy Alone

ChromaHorizon complements ChromaIndex benchmarks with the human support standards cannot replace.

Lived Experience First

No training program replaces someone who has walked the same path. Mentors are vetted for empathy, boundaries, and practical expertise.

Flexible by Design

Async messaging, optional video, and no penalty for pausing — built for minds that need predictability and low sensory load.

Standards + Support

Pair ChromaHorizon mentorship with ChromaIndex employer data to choose organizations worth your energy.
