About Chroma Hub International

We exist to re-engineer the systems that shape how people learn, work, and participate in civic life — making them universally accessible through psychological insight, open-source EdTech, and data-driven policy.

Our Mission

Chroma Hub International was founded on a simple but radical premise.

To re-engineer global education, workspaces, and civic assemblies into universally accessible environments by leveraging psychological insight, open-source EdTech, and data-driven corporate policy — framing human cognitive diversity not as a deficit, but as a vital spectrum of perspective.

What We Do

Our work operates across three interconnected domains of human experience.

Education

Building accessible classroom networks and advocacy incubators that empower learners of every cognitive profile to thrive.

Workplaces

Advancing neuro-inclusive hiring practices and peer-to-peer transition programs that reshape corporate culture from within.

Civic Life

Creating assemblies and public spaces where diverse minds can participate fully in democratic discourse and community building.

Our Values

Every project, partnership, and policy recommendation is guided by these core principles.

Universal Design

Environments should work for everyone from the start — not as an afterthought or accommodation.

Evidence-Based Practice

We ground our work in psychological research, open data, and measurable outcomes.

Open Source Commitment

Our EdTech tools and frameworks are built to be shared, adapted, and improved by communities worldwide.

Lived Experience Leadership

Neurodivergent voices are not consulted — they lead. Our governance reflects the communities we serve.

Intersectional Inclusion

Cognitive diversity intersects with race, gender, class, and geography. Our work addresses all of these dimensions.

Global Perspective

Inclusion looks different across cultures. We build frameworks that adapt to local context while sharing global standards.
