Project C

ChromaShare

The Accessible Classroom Network

ChromaShare is a global network connecting educators who are committed to universal design in the classroom. Teachers share resources, lesson plans, and accessibility strategies — building a living library of inclusive pedagogy that any educator can adapt.

  • Peer-to-peer resource sharing platform
  • Universal Design for Learning (UDL) templates
  • Multi-language support for global classrooms
  • Accessibility audit tools for existing curricula
  • Community forums moderated by neurodivergent educators
  • Integration with open-source EdTech tools

Project D

ChromaForge

The Asynchronous Advocacy Incubator

ChromaForge nurtures emerging advocates through self-paced programs designed for diverse cognitive profiles. Whether you process information best through reading, video, audio, or interactive modules — ChromaForge meets you where you are and helps you build the skills to drive change.

  • Self-paced advocacy training modules
  • Multi-modal content delivery (text, audio, visual, interactive)
  • Mentorship matching with experienced advocates
  • Project-based learning with real-world impact
  • Flexible deadlines and sensory-friendly design
  • Certificate programs recognized by partner organizations
