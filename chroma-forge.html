ChromaForge

ChromaForge nurtures emerging advocates through self-paced programs designed for diverse cognitive profiles. Whether you process information best through reading, video, audio, or interactive modules — ChromaForge meets you where you are and helps you build the skills to drive change.

Program Capabilities

Advocacy training built for how you actually learn — not how traditional programs assume you should.

Core

Self-Paced Advocacy Modules

Move through advocacy fundamentals on your own timeline. Each module breaks complex policy and community skills into clear, completable units you can pause and resume anytime.

  • Foundations of neuro-inclusive advocacy
  • School, workplace, and civic tracks
  • Checkpoint saves across devices
Multi-Modal

Learn Your Way

Every module ships in text, audio, visual, and interactive formats. Switch modes mid-lesson without losing progress — because one size never fits all minds.

  • Transcripts and captions on all media
  • Interactive scenario branches
  • Adjustable pacing and playback speed
Mentorship

Matched Advocate Guides

Pair with experienced advocates who understand your context — student, educator, parent, or community organizer — for async check-ins and practical feedback.

  • Interest and identity-aware matching
  • Structured mentorship milestones
  • Optional group cohort circles
Impact

Project-Based Learning

Apply skills to a real advocacy project in your school, workplace, or community. Capstone work is reviewed for clarity, accessibility, and measurable outcomes.

  • Guided project scaffolds
  • Peer review circles
  • Portfolio-ready deliverables
Access

Flexible & Sensory-Friendly

No surprise deadlines, no flashing progress bars, no penalty for pausing. ChromaForge is designed for cognitive and sensory diversity from the interface up.

  • Extension requests without justification
  • Reduced-motion and low-stimulation modes
  • Clear weekly rhythm, not daily pressure
Credentials

Recognized Certificates

Complete a track and earn a certificate recognized by Chroma partner organizations — evidence of advocacy skill for schools, employers, and grant applications.

  • Verifiable digital credentials
  • Track-specific specialization badges
  • Continuing education credit pathways

Advocacy Tracks

Track

How ChromaForge Works

From first module to certified advocate — on your terms.

1. Choose Your Mode

Pick a track and select how you want each module delivered — read, listen, watch, or interact. Your preference saves for next time.

2. Learn & Practice

Work through self-paced units, meet your mentor at agreed intervals, and build toward a capstone project with real-world impact.

3. Forge Change

Submit your project, earn your certificate, and join the Chroma advocate network — ready to mentor the next cohort.

Designed for Diverse Minds

ChromaForge is part of Chroma Academy's promise: advocacy training that includes the advocates it serves.
