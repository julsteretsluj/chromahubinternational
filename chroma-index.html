Benchmark Framework

Neuro-inclusion measured across the full employee lifecycle — not just hiring slogans.

Framework

Standardized Assessment

A consistent neuro-inclusion rubric applied to every organization — covering policies, practices, culture signals, and outcomes that affect neurodivergent employees.

  • Evidence-based scoring criteria
  • Self-assessment and verified audit paths
  • Comparable scores across organizations
Transparency

Public Annual Rankings

Rankings update every year with methodology published in full. Leaders earn visibility; laggards face accountability from candidates, investors, and employees.

  • Global and regional leaderboards
  • Year-over-year progress tracking
  • Methodology open for peer review
Sectors

Industry-Specific Benchmarks

Compare like with like. Sector profiles for tech, education, healthcare, and government reflect the hiring realities and accommodation norms of each field.

  • Tech & knowledge work
  • Education & public institutions
  • Healthcare & government
Roadmaps

Actionable Improvement Plans

Every assessment ships with a prioritized roadmap — quick wins, structural fixes, and long-term culture shifts mapped to your current score.

  • Recruitment & job design fixes
  • Onboarding & accommodation playbooks
  • Retention & advancement strategies
Verification

Third-Party Audit Process

Organizations pursuing verified status submit documentation and undergo independent review by trained auditors with neuro-inclusion expertise.

  • Documented evidence requirements
  • Independent auditor certification
  • Verified badge on public profiles
Reporting

ESG & DEI Integration

ChromaIndex scores map to ESG and DEI reporting frameworks so neuro-inclusion becomes a standard line item — not an afterthought in sustainability disclosures.

  • Exportable metrics for annual reports
  • Alignment with major DEI standards
  • Investor-ready inclusion summaries

Five Stages of Evaluation

Where inclusion breaks down — and where leaders pull ahead.

Stage 1

Recruitment

Accessible hiring from first touch

Job descriptions, application flows, interview formats, and assessment design — scored for cognitive accessibility and bias reduction before a candidate ever receives an offer.

Stage 2

Onboarding

First 90 days that include

Orientation materials, manager training, accommodation request pathways, and buddy programs — evaluated for clarity, predictability, and sensory load.

Stages 3–5

Accommodation, Retention & Advancement

Beyond the hire

How organizations handle adjustment requests, prevent burnout, promote neurodivergent talent, and measure inclusion outcomes over time — not just at the point of recruitment.

How ChromaIndex Works

From baseline assessment to public recognition.

1. Assess

Complete the standardized self-assessment or commission a verified third-party audit across all five lifecycle stages.

2. Improve

Receive your score, sector comparison, and a prioritized roadmap with concrete policy and practice recommendations.

3. Report & Rank

Opt into public rankings, export ESG-aligned metrics, and demonstrate year-over-year progress to stakeholders.
