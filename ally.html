Pillar 3 — Corporate & Policy

Chroma Ally

Our corporate compliance and global policy arm drives systemic change in hiring practices and workplace culture — using data, peer support, and evidence-based policy frameworks.

Projects

Transforming workplaces through measurable standards and human-centered transition support.

ChromaIndex

ChromaIndex is the world's first comprehensive benchmark for neuro-inclusive hiring practices. Organizations are evaluated across recruitment, onboarding, accommodation, retention, and advancement — with public rankings that drive accountability and celebrate leaders in cognitive inclusion.

  • Standardized neuro-inclusion assessment framework
  • Public company rankings updated annually
  • Sector-specific benchmarks (tech, education, healthcare, government)
  • Actionable improvement roadmaps for employers
  • Third-party verification and audit process
  • Integration with ESG and DEI reporting standards

ChromaHorizon

ChromaHorizon pairs neurodivergent professionals with peer mentors who have navigated similar career transitions. Whether entering the workforce, changing roles, or advocating for accommodations — participants receive personalized, lived-experience guidance through every stage of their professional journey.

  • One-on-one peer mentorship matching
  • Career transition playbooks by industry
  • Accommodation request templates and coaching
  • Employer education sessions led by neurodivergent professionals
  • Community support groups with flexible participation formats
  • Success stories and case study library
