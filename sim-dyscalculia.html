How this simulation works
  1. Complete 4 steps — each one asks you to judge quantity using numbers or symbols.
  2. In challenge mode, digits scramble, labels lie, and random symbols float across the screen.
  3. The symptom log names what breaks magnitude sense as you interact.
  4. Use Spatial Pivot to redo tasks with containers, pitch, and bars — no raw numbers.
