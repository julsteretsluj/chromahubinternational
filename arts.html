Chroma Arts

Our global advocacy and arts collective amplifies neurodivergent voices through creative expression — because the fight for inclusion is also a fight for culture, representation, and beauty.

Projects

Where advocacy meets artistry — changing hearts and minds through creative expression.

ChromaPulse

ChromaPulse is a worldwide collective of neurodivergent artists, writers, musicians, filmmakers, and activists. Through exhibitions, performances, publications, and digital campaigns, ChromaPulse makes cognitive diversity visible — challenging stereotypes and celebrating the creative power of different minds.

  • Global artist residency and fellowship programs
  • Annual ChromaPulse Festival (hybrid in-person and virtual)
  • Open-access digital gallery and media archive
  • Collaborative advocacy campaigns across social platforms
  • Micro-grants for neurodivergent creative projects
  • Partnerships with museums, galleries, and cultural institutions
  • Storytelling workshops for self-advocacy through art

Art as Advocacy

Creative Expression Without Barriers

From street art in São Paulo to digital installations in Seoul, ChromaPulse connects creative advocates across borders.
